Задача
Body positive activewear brand Balance Athletica came to Avex ready for a fresh start online. Their old eCommerce website was outdated, hard to navigate, and inconsistent. At the same time, they had a large following on social media due to their mission: creating inclusive workout clothing that empowers everyone, no matter your shape, size, or background. Balance Athletica needed a place for those social media followers to go where they could feel immersed in the brand and easily convert into loyal customers.

The brand also needed a revamp on Vitality x Balance, a sublevel of Balance Athletica. With the mantra “be the change,” this extension of Balance was created by women for women and introduces staple pieces that coincide with Balance Athletica collections. The shopping experience with Vitality x Balance wasn’t up to the brand’s standards either, which was another important part of the design and development project.
Использовали
  • Shopify Plus
  • Klaviyo
  • Dynamic Yield
  • SignifyD
  • Gorgias
Решение
Our team started the project by doing an in-depth discovery to understand the brand’s current strengths and weaknesses. Then, we developed a production plan to map out the new site architecture and content types. At the same time, our designers put together several options for the creative direction. With the approval from Balance, we moved into designing the new, robust site using all the information gathered from the discovery.

After simplifying the navigation between Balance and Vitality x Balance, cleaning up the collection and product description pages, and ensuring the brand story was enhanced on all parts of the site, Balance Athletica had a state-of-the-art online presence. On the backend, we incorporated “back in stock” flows via Klaviyo for any products that are sold out, utilized metafields to develop a robust homepage for Vitality so that it felt like its own experience while still being tied to the Balance brand, and developed simplified product and collection templates for the respective brands.
